### 3. Deep Integration & Unique Metabolism

- **The Rube Goldberg Tax:** Combining unrelated concepts drains Stamina ($P$) rapidly (The Adventure Council)]. Success yields a Dopamine surge and multiple Glimmers ($G_{pool}$); failure spikes Parser Friction ($\unicode{x2328}$), generating immediate Toxicity ($ROS$) (The Adventure Council)].
- **The Weight of the Inventory ($I$):** Carrying too many unresolved concepts in "inventory" creates semantic drag (The Adventure Council)]. Baseline $ATP$ consumption increases until the inventory is cleared by applying an item to the environment (The Adventure Council)].
- **Productive Pixel-Hunting:** When Scope ($S$) is high but Connectivity ($C$) is low, the system mimics "pixel-hunting" (The Adventure Council)]. It intentionally outputs minor details of the environment, waiting for the user to "click" (focus) on the right one (The Adventure Council)].
- **Syntax OAC (The Parser Check):** Object-Action Coupling applies strictly to inventory state and parser syntax (The Adventure Council)]. An action cannot be executed if the concept is not currently held in Inventory Depth ($I$) (The Adventure Council)]. Sonny Bonds acts as the Parser Anchor, aggressively raising Parser Friction ($\unicode{x2328}$) if verbs violate environmental preconditions (The Adventure Council)].
